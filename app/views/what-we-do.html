Characteristics of ITN Service

Available 7 days a week, 24 hours a day for any purpose
No money is exchanged in the vehicle, and riders pre-fund a personal transportation account
Rides are provided in private automobiles by trained drivers, no vans or buses
We welcome members that are 60 years and older or who have disabilities
Rides may be booked with discounts
Riders may travel alone or with others, with 20% discounts for shared rides
Community-based affiliates are supported by private, rather than public resources
ITNs are non-profit, keeping fares affordable
Not "just a taxi" - drivers provide arm-through-arm, door-to-door service and help with packages
The CarTrade™ program allows you to trade your car for rides
The HealthyMiles™ program allows businesses and health cares providers to participate
The Ride & Shop™ program allows you to receive discount rates at certain businesses

Dignified

The Independent Transportation Network® (ITN) is dedicated to providing dignified transportation. Older adults join ITNOrlando and become dues-paying members of an organization committed to their mobility. When they pick up the telephone to schedule a ride, they are not asking a favor. ITNOrlando is a nonprofit built to ease the transition from the driver's seat to the passenger seat.

Consumer Oriented

At ITNOrlando, we provide arm-through-arm, door-to-door service, a standard that sets us apart from taxis and other transportation services. And like using your own automobile, ITN is available 24/7, offering the flexibility to schedule rides in advance or as needed, as well as the option to ride alone or with others. Because our members aren’t just our riders, they’re also our customers.

Call to find out if you live in our service area

We are a membership-based organization. Our annual membership fee covers up to two members of the same household either over 60 years of age or adults with visual impairment 18 years and older.
